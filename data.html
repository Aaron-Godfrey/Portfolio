This is the model

I set out with goal of determining any key factors that would influence the rating of a given movie, in hopes that I could develop a general rule of thumb to follow for marketing to correct demographics. The dataset, courtesy of MovieLens, was comprised of 1,000,209 anonymous ratings of 3,900 different movies that also contained data for various facets of the users and movies, such as gender and age of reviewer and genre of movie. My attempt to find a correlation utilized a multinomial logistic regression approach to hopefully filter out any interactions that were insignificant to prediction, however I concluded that no clear general factors had impacts on the review scores. If I were to reattempt this analysis, I would consider creating a neural network as to hopefully catch more fine-tuned interaction terms that would lead to a more significant rating prediction.
